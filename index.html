What is speakly about

Speakly is an online language exchange platform where users connect to practice speaking, listening, and even reading in a variety of languages. The focus is on real, conversational practice, allowing users to pick up not only grammar and vocabulary but also the nuances, idioms, and cultural elements that are so crucial to fluency.
